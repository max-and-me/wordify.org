Why use Wordify?

Wordify is an ARA / VST 3 plug-in. It transcribes (speech to text) selected clips from the project in a chat-like manner. Clicking on a word, locates to the time position where the word is spoken. A smart search makes it possible to quickly find spoken words in clips.

Features

Selection

  • Locate your project by simply clicking on words
  • DAW colors of clips are used for easy recognizability
  • Waveform follows the current selection
  • When you work on clips Wordify will follow

Smart Search

  • When you get it wrong Wordify will get it right for you
  • Smooth visualization of found results
  • Effortless navigation

Sleek UI Design

  • Light and dark scheme for best readibility in all environments
  • Non-blocking user interface through background analysis
  • Optimized for Apple Silicon
